## Data

The dataset for this project is sourced from [Kaggle's consumer analytics data](URL), which I have filtered and cleaned based on specific requirements for analyzing Brand X's electronics category. The data includes detailed information on product attributes, customer demographics, transaction history, return rates, and promotional channels.

---

## Business Questions Addressed

This project seeks to answer the following key business questions:

1. **What Product Features Have Driven High Consumer Engagement for Brand X in Electronics?**
   - Purpose: Identify which product attributes (e.g., color, size, material) have resonated with consumers to help prioritize features in the new product design.

2. **Which Products in the Electronics Category for Brand X Are Showing the Highest Sales Growth Over Time?**
   - Purpose: Identify products experiencing significant growth in sales, allowing Brand X to incorporate popular features or capitalize on trends in the upcoming product launch.

3. **What is the Return Rate for Electronics Products by Brand X, and Are There Any Common Issues?**
   - Purpose: Assess consumer satisfaction by analyzing return rates and identifying potential quality or fit issues related to specific product features.

4. **Which Promotion Channels and Types Have Been Most Effective in Driving Engagement for Brand X's Electronics?**
   - Purpose: Determine effective promotional strategies to replicate successful approaches for the new product launch.

5. **Which Customer Segments Generate the Highest Average Order Value (AOV) for Brand X's Electronics?**
   - Purpose: Identify demographics that tend to make higher-value purchases, allowing Brand X to target premium offerings toward high-value customers.

## Key Findings and Recommendation 

### 1. High-Engagement Product Features for Brand X's Electronics

1. **Optimal Size - Medium**:
   - Medium-sized products generate **33.5% of total sales** and have the highest engagement, with over **11 million reviews**. This suggests that Medium size meets consumer expectations in functionality and design.
   - **Recommendation**: Prioritize Medium size for the new product to align with strong consumer preference.

2. **Preferred Color - Red**:
   - Red-colored products capture **20.2% of sales** and achieve the highest rating at **3.01**. This color resonates strongly, with over **6.6 million reviews** indicating high satisfaction.
   - **Recommendation**: Include Red in the product design to leverage its appeal.

3. **Top Material - Wood**:
   - Wood accounts for **25.2% of total sales** and draws **8.3 million reviews** with a steady rating. Consumers appreciate Wood, likely for its quality or look.
   - **Recommendation**: Consider Wood as a preferred material for new products.

### 2. Top-Selling Products with Consistent Growth for Brand X's Electronics

1. **Strong Growth Performers**:
   - **Product A** demonstrated the highest overall growth, with a **3.26% total QoQ increase** across 2020 and 2021. Notably, Q3 2021 showed a **7.24% increase**, indicating strong seasonal demand.
   - **Product D** also showed positive growth with an overall **2.35% QoQ increase**, driven by consistent performance in Q2 and Q4 2021, where growth exceeded **5%** in both quarters.

2. **Declining Product**:
   - **Product B** experienced a **4.41% overall decline**, with multiple quarters of negative growth. This suggests a decrease in consumer interest or potential issues with market positioning.

3. **Moderate Growth**:
   - **Product C** remained relatively stable with **0.24% total growth**. Although it experienced fluctuations, it rebounded with a slight increase in Q4 2021.

   **Recommendation**: For the new product launch, Brand X should incorporate features and attributes aligned with **Products A and D** to capitalize on their upward trend and seasonal appeal. Product B’s decline indicates it may need a refreshed approach or reconsideration in the lineup.

### 3. Return Rates and Product Issues

1. **Overall Return Rate**: Brand X's electronics have an average return rate of **25.06%**, which serves as a benchmark for identifying potential areas of improvement.

2. **Promotion Types**:
   - **Buy One Get One Free** promotions have the highest return rate at **25.12%**, slightly above the average, suggesting consumers may feel differently about products when they are part of promotional bundles.
   - **Flash Sales** have a marginally lower return rate at **24.99%**, indicating slightly better satisfaction with products purchased under time-limited offers.

3. **Customer Demographics**:
   - **Income Bracket**: High-income consumers show a higher return rate of **25.17%** compared to low-income (25.03%) and medium-income (25.00%) brackets, which may indicate higher expectations in product quality.
   - **Gender**: Male customers have a marginally higher return rate at **25.12%**, suggesting possible variances in expectations or usage patterns.

4. **Price Sensitivity**:
   - Products priced between **$200-$300** and **$800-$900** show slightly elevated return rates at **25.30%** and **25.23%** respectively, which could reflect dissatisfaction with value at these price points.

   **Recommendation**: To reduce return rates, Brand X should focus on managing consumer expectations for higher-priced items and consider refining promotions, particularly for "Buy One Get One Free" offers.

### 4. Effective Promotion Channels and Types for Driving Engagement by Customer Type

1. **Promotional Channels**:
   - **In-store Promotions**: Ideal for **returning customers**, who have the highest average purchase value of **$256.19**. This channel is effective for building loyalty and maximizing spending among existing customers.
   - **Social Media**: Best suited for **new customers**, with an average purchase value of **$254.45**. Social media promotions effectively attract new customers and encourage initial engagement, making it a strong channel for customer acquisition.

2. **Promotional Types**:
   - **20% Off Promotion**: Highly effective for **returning customers**, who spend an average of **$256.66** under this promotion. Offering a discount encourages repeat purchases, making it a valuable tool for customer retention.
   - **Flash Sales**: Engages **new customers** with an average purchase value of **$255.15**. Flash sales create urgency and are effective for attracting new buyers looking for quick deals.

### 5. High-Value Customer Segments for Brand X's Electronics

1. **Income Bracket**:
   - Customers in the **Medium income bracket** have the highest average order value (AOV) at **$1,014.84**, followed closely by those in the Low income bracket at **$1,013.37**.
   - **High-income customers** have a lower AOV of **$1,006.35**, suggesting that mid-range products may resonate more with the broader customer base.

   **Recommendation**: Focus on medium-priced products and promotions for **Medium and Low income** segments, as they show a higher willingness to spend per transaction.

2. **Age and Gender Segments** (from previous insights):
   - Older age groups (54+) and Male customers have shown higher AOVs, indicating that combining these demographics with targeted income segments could enhance revenue.

   **Overall Strategy**: Brand X should prioritize **medium-priced products** for **Medium and Low income** customers and emphasize premium offerings for **older, high-value segments** (54+ age group and Male customers) to maximize profitability across key demographic groups.

## Conclusion

This analysis provides Brand X with a clear roadmap for optimizing its upcoming electronics product launch. By prioritizing **high-engagement features** (Medium size, Red color, and Wood material) and leveraging effective **promotion channels** (in-store for returning customers and social media for new ones), Brand X can maximize product appeal and customer engagement. Targeting **older age groups** and **Male customers** with premium offerings, while focusing medium-priced promotions on **Medium and Low income** segments, will further enhance profitability. These insights ensure that Brand X's product and marketing strategies are aligned with consumer preferences, driving stronger market performance and customer satisfaction.
